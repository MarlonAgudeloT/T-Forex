Forex Trading

Currency trading is the act of buying and selling currencies. It is the largest financial market in the world with a daily turnover of $5 trillion and involves many people and many currencies. As you are always buying one currency with another currency, you trade "currency pairs".

Â¿What is Forex?

The foreign exchange market, also known as Forex (Foreign Exchange) is a global, decentralized market where currencies are traded. Each of these currencies has a conversion rate, called an exchange rate. For example, if the EUR/USD has an exchange rate of 1.10, it means that 1 euro is equivalent to 1.10 US dollars. Forex is one of the most liquid markets, where the daily volume of transactions is 5 trillion dollars a day. It is mainly used to facilitate transactions and investments between countries. The Forex market is open 24 hours a day except weekends, opening in Sydney on Monday mornings and closing in New York on Friday afternoons.

Trading Types

Fundamental Analysis

Fundamental analysis is a method used to assess the intrinsic value of an asset and to analyze the factors that could influence its price in the future. This type of analysis is based on the evaluation of assets based on external facts and influences, as well as financial statements and industry trends. Summary

Technical Analysis

Technical analysis is a system for examining and predicting price movements in financial markets based on historical data and market statistics. It is based on the idea that if an investor can identify previous patterns, then he will be able to predict future price movements fairly accurately. Summary

Choosing a Forex Broker

Before trading, you need to open an account with a Forex broker. What is a broker? In simple terms it is an intermediary between you and the foreign exchange market. Brokers earn money by charging a commission every time a trade is opened in the market. The broker should preferably be regulated in one of the major financial markets. Must have a good customer service. Competitive costs in the market. Summary

Practice on a demo account

A demo account is a type of trading account, through which you can trade in the market under real conditions, but with virtual money. In this way, beginner traders can have a real experience without putting their capital at risk. It is recommended to trade on a demo account for at least one month before opening a real money account. Summary

Money Management

Good money management is the key to a profitable investment in the Forex market. There are some rules you should stick to, but in addition to following them you should adapt what is known as money management to your trading style. What does money management entail? It is mainly about determining the size of the position together with the risk taken. Summary
